# NLP Review Mining — Google Play Store Apps (Zepto vs Instamart)

## Project Overview

This project focuses on building a **Python NLP pipeline** to extract, clean, and analyse **Google Play Store reviews** for **Zepto** and **Instamart** to map sentiment trends and recurring experience themes.

The analysis was designed to compare customer sentiment across two leading quick-commerce apps, identifying what drives positive experiences and where users face friction. Through this analysis, the pipeline processed **5,000+ reviews**, ranked **fast delivery as the #1 positive driver**, and flagged **refunds and product quality as the most frequent pain points**. It also uncovered distinct sentiment distributions, recurring topic clusters, and classification-based experience drivers that inform product and customer experience strategy.

## Project back ground

- The Google Play Store hosts thousands of user reviews for quick-commerce apps like Zepto and Instamart, containing unstructured feedback on delivery speed, product quality, refunds, and overall experience.

- The goal of this project was to transform raw review text into structured insights that could answer practical business questions such as what drives positive ratings, where users face friction, and how the two apps compare on customer sentiment.

- This analysis helps translate unstructured voice-of-customer data into performance insights useful for product strategy, customer experience improvements, and competitive positioning.

- The project was structured to extract reviews, preprocess text, run sentiment analysis, apply topic modeling, and build classification models so that experience drivers and pain points could be identified clearly.

***

### Questions answered through the analysis

- What is the overall sentiment distribution for Zepto vs Instamart?
- Which topics and themes recur most frequently in user reviews?
- What is the #1 positive driver across both apps?
- What are the most frequent pain points mentioned by users?
- How do sentiment trends differ between Zepto and Instamart?
- What percentage of reviews mention delivery speed, refunds, or product quality?
- Which app has a higher concentration of negative feedback on specific issues?
- Where should product and CX teams focus their improvement efforts?

## Analysis design approach

- The analysis was designed to present insights in a simple and decision-oriented format.

## Key Insights

- Processed **5,000+ Google Play Store reviews** across Zepto and Instamart
- Ranked **fast delivery as the #1 positive driver**, emerging as the top factor driving positive sentiment
- Flagged **refunds and product quality as the most frequent pain points**, indicating critical friction areas
- Mapped distinct **sentiment trends** showing how user perception varies between the two apps
- Identified **recurring experience themes** through topic modeling, revealing clusters around delivery, product quality, refunds, and app usability
- Built **classification models** to systematically rank drivers of positive and negative experiences
- Delivered **structured CX insights** to support product prioritization and customer experience strategy
